English

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data in the Aaref Sandwich app at any time. This page explains how to delete your account and what data will be removed.

1 Delete From Within the App

  1. Open the Aaref Sandwich app.
  2. Tap the menu (☰) and choose Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm, and your account and data will be deleted.

2 Delete by Contacting Us

If you can't delete in-app, send a request from your registered email or phone, and we'll delete your account within 30 days:

Emailsandwtsharf@gmail.com
Phone+966 50 290 2055

3 Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Registered phone number.
  • Past orders and their data.
  • Saved addresses, loyalty points, and wallet.
  • All personal data linked to the account.

We may retain some data for a limited period if required by law (e.g. invoice records), after which it is permanently deleted.
